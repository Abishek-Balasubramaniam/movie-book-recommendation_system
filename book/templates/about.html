Recommendation systems:

Recommender systems are beneficial to both service providers and users in an online shopping and content consuming (Youtube, Netflix, etc.,) environment. Recommendation systems also improve the decision-making process.

What is Content Based Recommendation?

In the content recommender system, the content of the movie (overview, cast, crew, keyword, tagline etc) is used to find its similarity with that of the books. Then the books that are most likely to be similar are recommended.

Importance of Recommendation systems

As stated earlier, Recommendation systems have been beneficial in online shopping and content consuming environments. Recommender systems are really critical in some industries as they can generate a huge amount of income when they are efficient or also be a way to stand out significantly from competitors.
